(Multi-)Vector DDoS Attack Labeler

A practical way to label DDoS attacks within packet-based network traces.

About

From a network perspective, a DDoS attack is set of packets with simillar characteristics sent by more than one source IP addresses. Nowadays attacks, instead of being composed of a single set of attack characteristics (single attack vector), it has being composed by multiple sets of characteristics, multi-vector attacks.

This initiative is lidered by the Nationale Beheersorganisatie Internet Providers (NBIP) and under the scientific and technical supervision and development of Jair Santanna. Available at his GitHub repository to facilitate the re-usage and the improvement proposed by the community. You can also read the post that describes our approach.
